Introducing the ST-13 By astéron

Everything a student-needs- wants

 






DESIGN

First of it's 
Class

With full Steilness Steel body 
and a 360° hinge with astéron
pencil, this design is the first of it’s 
class. The design is inspired from a 
notebook, so it feels familiar to write
on. With an astéron pencil, and a matte
finished display with touch and astéron
pencil input, it feels like writing on a regular
notebook that will never run out of storage.




Slimmest Laptop Ever

Period

At just 450gms and 0.8cms thick, the ST-13 is the
Slimmest Laptop Ever. It’s so light and slim that it feels
like you are holding a real notebook. The ultra light.              

weight makes it easier to keep on your lap and complete
your homework without any fatigue as
it’s so slim, that it feels like it’s not ever
there. The ultra slim body with matte
finish on the display makes it easier
to write with a breeze using our 
astéron pencil that is included 
with the device.






DISPLAY

Feels Like Real
Life

With a 13 inch 360hz SuperAmoled+ Display
with Touch and astéron pencil input and a 
4k resolution, this display feels like Real Life.
With the matte glass display, the glass is as
same as a real notebook, so it feels like
writing on a real paper with the astéron
pencil. The SuperAmoled+ display with
360hz refresh rate gives the best contrast
ratio with realistic blacks that gives rest to 
your eyes and is so smooth that it feels like
real life.






HINGE

Best Hinge Ever

With a 360 degree rotatable
hinge that can stop at any angle,
the ST-13 has the best hinge ever
on a laptop. The hinge can stop
at any angle you want with a firm. 
fit and will never wobble on its
own. The 360 degree hinge is
able to lie the laptop flat with
no space between so it feels like
writing on a flat paper with our
astéron pencil.

ASTÈRON PENCIL

Better than Real Pencil

With the body made from brushed Steilness Steel
with a matte finished nib, the astéron pencil feels
more realistic than real life. The matte finished nib
gives the resistence and feel of a real pencil while
writing on the matte finished display of the ST-13.
The Brushed Steilness Steel body makes it
easier to hold and is sweat resistant
so it wont get fatiguing after hours
of homework. The RGB illuminated
nib and back helps it easier to see and work in
the dark, so you will able to complete your school
work even if it gets late.




And yes, it's RGB              

       

                                            
With RGB illuminated nib and back, you
can choose any color you want. The
RGB will help you to use and locate the

astéron pencil easily in the dark without
any problems. You can even choose
any animation you want, choose from
our pre-built presets or make your own!





Easily Rechargeable with
Long Lasting battery

The astéron pencil is easily rechargable
with Lightning USB connector that
provides charging speed to upto
45 Watt which can charge your pencil’s
battery from 0% to 100% in just
15 minutes! With a 1200 mAh Battery,
the astéron pencil will last for upto
260 days with a single charge, so you
won’t have to charge it again and
again. The RGB illumination will glow
Red when the battery is about to
discharge so you know when it’s time
to charge it again.
